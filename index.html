IoT based Adolescent Dilemma Handler is an one portal solution for problems which youths faces in day to day life.

Summary.

IoT based ‘Adolescents dilemma’s factotum’
IoT (Internet Of Things) based JAVA Swing MVC application to digitalize monitoring/handling adolescent dilemmas. Implemented Scalable One-portal solution for healthcare, financial, and academic anxieties using ecosystem model. Integrated predictive analysis with implicit access of nearby medical professional’s geolocation on patient phone. Introduced group messenger, online chat portal forums and notification service for one-click appointments, and providing solutions without impersonal meet. Other features: GPS tracking, emotion gauge, password Strength, JFreeCharts analysis.

About

In present scenario, younger generations are did not have time for taking out personally connect with professionals including Professors, doctors, mentors etc. They cannot even fetch out Community financial services to utilizes complimentary services and budget. With built-in portal, online appointments, group chat portals, automated financial approvals, online healthcare will enable student to have spare time to enjoy their lives with friends and family.
